KleverList is the ultimate solution for WooCommerce store owners who want to supercharge their email marketing efforts and achieve more effective customer segmentation.

== Description ==

#### SUPERCHARGE YOUR EMAIL MARKETING CAMPAIGNS

With KleverList, you can seamlessly synchronize your WooCommerce customer data with your favorite email marketing platform, allowing you to easily access all the crucial information you need to create targeted and personalized email campaigns. Our plugin takes care of everything, automatically pulling user and billing fields and sending them straight to your autoresponder lists.

But that's not all - with KleverList, you have complete control over your email marketing strategy. Our granular product assignment feature allows you to assign specific products to unique lists, enabling you to segment your customers based on their purchase history and behavior. Alternatively, you can specify a generic list for all products, ensuring that all your customers receive relevant and engaging content.

KleverList is the perfect tool for any WooCommerce store owner looking to streamline their email marketing efforts and achieve better results. Download our plugin today and take the first step towards supercharging your email marketing campaigns!

#### INTEGRATIONS:
* Sendy


#### FEATURES:
* Easy integration with API keys
* You can easily assign every WooCommerce product to a specific or to a generic list
* WooCommerce customer details get integrated in the lists for extreme segmentation
* You can granularly choose which information get sent to the autoresponder

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/kleverlist` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings by going to the KleverList dashboard page and entering your Sendy API credentials.
4. Select the brand and generate a brand in dashboard page.
5. Select Sendy integration from the mapping page and choose your Sendy list.
6. Configure customer billing details on the mapping page of the plugin.
7. Create and send better newsletters.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Sendy? =

Sendy is a self-hosted email marketing software that allows you to send newsletters, track email opens, clicks, and more. Learn more about Sendy at https://sendy.co.

= How do I configure the plugin? =

Go to the KleverList dashboard page and enter your Sendy API credentials and choose the brand and generate the list.

= Can I assign a different Sendy list to each WooCommerce product? =

Yes, you can add assign a different sendy list to each and every separate woocommerce product, You just need to set a different list from each product details page in the product data tab option. You can see the KleverList tab in each product and choose the list.
